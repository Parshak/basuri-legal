Common questions

Does Bodhi connect to my bank?
No. Bodhi never connects to any bank, account, or network. You enter your balances by hand, and everything stays on your device.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Bodhi collects nothing and sends nothing anywhere. Your information lives only on your phone. See the Privacy Policy for details.
How do I check in?
Open the app and tap the check-in card. Enter the current balance for each account — the new number simply replaces the old one. Bodhi works out what flowed in and out for you.
I lost my data / I want to start over.
Because everything is stored only on your device, deleting the app removes your data. Inside the app, Settings includes a "Reset to fresh start" option if you'd like to begin again.
Is Bodhi financial advice?
No. Bodhi is a personal money tracker to help you stay aware of your money. It is not financial advice and not a banking service.
